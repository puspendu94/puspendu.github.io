1772

Governor of Bengal

Warren Hastings

1773

First Governor General of Bengal

GG: Warren Hastings
Regulating Act of 1773
Abolished the dual system of administration
Divided Bengal into districts and appointed collectors and other revenue officials.
Established India's first Supreme Court in Calcutta.

1774

First Rohilla War

GG: Warren Hastings
The First Rohilla War was a punitive campaign by Shuja-ud-Daula, Nawab of Awadh on the behalf of Mughal Emperor, against the Rohillas, Afghan highlanders settled in Rohilkhand, northern India. The Nawab was supported by troops of the British East India Company

1783

Edmund Burke Bill

GG: Warren Hastings

1784

Asiatic Society of Bengal

GG: Warren Hastings
Founded with William Jones
Intoduction to Gita by Charles Wilkins
Pitts India Act was passed
Started Diwani and Faujdari adalats and the district level Sadar diwani and Nizmat adalats (appellate courts)
Redefined Hindi and Muslim laws.
A translation of code in Sanskrit appeared under the title 'Code of Gentoo laws'.

1785

Last year of Warren Hastings

GG: Warren Hastings
Deprived zamindar of their judicial powers. Manintenance of records was made compulsory.
Impeachment proceedings started against him in Britain on the charges of taking bribes. After trial of 7 years, he was finally acquitted.
